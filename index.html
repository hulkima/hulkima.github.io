Haokai Ma 

Ph.D. Email: sdlcmahaokai [at] 163.com
I have received my Ph.D. and B.E. degree in Shandong University in June 2024 and June 2019, respectively. My research interests span cross-domain recommendation, diffusion-enhanced recommendation and multi-media analytics. I have more than 10 publications appeared in several top conferences such as AAAI, IJCAI, MM, Recsys, ICMR, IJCNN, and journals including TOIS, JBI, CVMJ and Journal of Software. Moreover, I have been invited as the TPC/PC member for AAAI, IJCAI, ACMMM and Recsys and the Session Chair for IJCAI and IJCNN.

We have released a survey on the negative sampling strategies of Recsys:
- Negative Sampling in Recommendation: A Survey and Future Directions
