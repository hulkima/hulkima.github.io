I am a Ph.D. candidate of MMRC Lab in School of Software, Shandong University. I received B.Eng. from Shandong University in July 2019. My research interests span cold-start recommendation, cross-domain recommendation and multi-media analytics. I have almost 10 publications appeared in several top conferences such as ICMR, ICME, IJCNN, CICAI, and journals including JBI. Moreover, I have been invited as the reviewer for AAAI 2022, IJCNN 2023 etc., and Technical Program Committee (TPC) member for IJCNN.
